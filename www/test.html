Arroz Caldo

Soothing hot soup with rice and chicken cook from ginger

Recipe:

1. Chop Garlic

2. Chop Ginger

3. Fish Sauce

4. Chop Chicken and chicken liver

5. Rice

a. Saute in garlic,ginger,fish sauce and chicken together for around 15-20 mins just to make sure the chicken is cooked well

b. Add 4 Cups of water together with the rice

c. Wait till the rice gets cook and put in some salt and pepper.

d. Serve while it is hot, enjoy!
